Grilled Burgers Recipe

Follow this simple recipe to create dad approved grilled burgers at home!

A simple recipe that will bring back summer-time memories of splashing in the pool while dad grilled burgers and hotdogs!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger buns
  • Ground beef
  • Mayo
  • American cheese

Steps

  1. Add salt to beef and form patties
  2. Spread mayo on burger buns
  3. Get grill fired up and ready
  4. Place buger patties on grill and cook to temp
  5. 1 minute before patties reach desired temp, cover pattie with a slice of American cheese
  6. Place buns mayo down on grill but indirect heat for about a minute
  7. Turn buns to toast the outside
  8. Take buns and patties off the grill and begin assembly
  9. Pop a cold one and enjoy!
