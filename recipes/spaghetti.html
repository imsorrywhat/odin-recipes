Lasagna Recipe

Follow this simple recipe to create mom approved spaghetti at home!

A simple recipe that will conjure up the nostalgia! Sitting around the dinner table with family and enjoying hot, flavorful, slurpy spaghetti!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook noodles
  2. Cook groud beef
  3. Add tomato sauce to cooked ground beef
  4. Combine cooked noodles with meat sauce
  5. Serve in a bowl and grate parmesan cheese over spaghetti
  6. Add crushed red pepper for some heat
