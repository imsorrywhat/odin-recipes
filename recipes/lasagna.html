Lasagna Recipe

Follow this simple recipe to create mom approved lasagna at home!

A simple recipe that will conjure up the nostalgia! Sitting around the dinner table with family and enjoying hot, flavorful, gooey lasagna fresh from the oven!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook noodles
  2. Cook groud beef
  3. Add tomato sauce to cooked ground beef
  4. Shred Mozzarella cheese
  5. Cover bottom of 9 x 13 pan with lasagna noodles
  6. Cover first layer with meat sauce
  7. Cover meat sauce with shredded Mozzarella cheese
  8. Repeat Steps 5-7 until pan is filled or Ingredients are used up
  9. Bake in oven for 50 minutes or until top is golden brown
